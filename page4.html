Grants and Awards

The Duolingo English Test advances testing for good by funding grants and fellowships to support future assessment researchers. Learn about our research governance here.

Competitive Research Grants

The Competitive Research Grants are intended to support topics of interest related to Duolingo English Test’s ongoing research agenda. Up to three research grants will be awarded and each study will receive up to USD $120,000 based on the scope of the study.
